This open source app reads subscan and tries to collect your total rewards from the last week (28 eras) for each validator you currenty nominate, and display it as a percentage. The idea is to provide a bit of info on how your nomination mix is working. Nominumbers runs in your browser, no data is collected - you can also download the zip and use it locally on your device (see the README for more info). GPL3 source code here.
